# Character Roster (LOCKED + pressure-tested)

**20 characters, cast locked**, then stress-tested against the four-framework constraint — see [`pressure-test-01.md`](pressure-test-01.md) for the audit and the fixes folded in below. Organized by **stake and relationship**, spread across class / age / belief, with a living human **champion for every framework** so the mystery never collapses.

## The four frameworks (must all stay plausible — §6, §19.13)

| Key | Possibility |
|---|---|
| **A** | **One real human** who fiercely protected their identity. |
| **B** | **A passed-down mantle** — the byline inherited by several people, *Dread Pirate Roberts* style. |
| **C** | **A rogue AI** faking a human, having planted years of breadcrumbs. |
| **D** | **Never existed** — an emergent myth assembled from unrelated anonymous activity and coincidence. |

## ⚠ Hard rule: no verifiable relationship with the human

> **No player character has a verifiable, in-person relationship with the human behind the byline.** Any provable human relationship would instantly kill Frameworks **C** and **D**. Every apparent tie is **mediated / remembered / inherited / fabricated**. The people who *feel* closest are the ones who cannot prove a body ever existed.

## The 20
