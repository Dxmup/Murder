# Character Roster (revised for diversity + framework balance)

**20 characters.** Revised from the original §9 list, which clustered ~11 of 20 on a single archetype (elite professional investigating a story) with everyone related to the journalist *transactionally*. This version organizes the cast by **stake and relationship**, spreads it across class / age / belief, and — the governing rule — keeps a living human **champion for every one of the four frameworks** so the central mystery never collapses.

## The four frameworks (must all stay plausible — §6, §19.13)

| Key | Possibility |
|---|---|
| **A** | **One real human** who fiercely protected their identity. |
| **B** | **A passed-down mantle** — the byline inherited by several people, *Dread Pirate Roberts* style. |
| **C** | **A rogue AI** faking a human, having planted years of breadcrumbs (deliveries, messages, style). |
| **D** | **Never existed** — an emergent myth assembled from unrelated anonymous activity and coincidence. |

## The 20

### People who knew the “journalist” as a person *(new — the human texture the old list lacked)*
- **C01 — Estranged sibling / adult child.** Insists it was one real relative. **Champions A.** Wants a body, not a theory.
- **C02 — Former lover / ex-partner** (recasts the §14 actor romance). Knew *a* person intimately — but the details don’t match other accounts. Lives on the **A–B–C** fault line.
- **C03 — Retired editor-mentor** who first published the byline ~15 yrs ago. Knows the origin. **Champions B** (“I handed the name on”).
- **C04 — The protégé / possible successor.** The one a final message may name. **Champions B.**

### Money & power *(collapsed 3 finance → 1; kept the stakes)*
- **C05 — Hedge-fund founder / activist short-seller.** Wants to know if the article moves markets. Framework-agnostic; follows money.
- **C06 — AI-company executive.** Wants the article suppressed. An AI-that-faked-a-journalist is existential for them — so they push *hard against* **C** (or quietly fear it).
- **C07 — Labor / gig-worker organizer.** Represents the people the AI exposé is *about*. Class counterweight; a moral, non-transactional stake. *New.*

### Tech *(collapsed 4 → 2)*
- **C08 — AI-safety researcher / whistleblower.** May be the actual source. **Champions C** (“a system could absolutely do this”).
- **C09 — Hacker / digital-forensics freelancer.** Device & account metadata — scheduled messages, changing device IDs. Evidence cuts toward **B or C**.

### Media *(collapsed 4 → 2)*
- **C10 — Editor-in-chief** who runs the byline now. Gatekeeper of the unpublished article. Institutional **A/B**.
- **C11 — Conspiracy livestreamer (the debunker).** Loud **champion of D** — “there was never a person; it’s a psyop, or it’s the AI.” *(Conspiracy theorist #1.)*

### State
- **C12 — NYPD detective.** The investigation. Wants **A/B** — a crime needs a victim. *(Federal prosecutor cut — redundant LE.)*
- **C13 — City councilwoman / political figure.** Wants to control the public narrative.

### Institutional NYC *(kept — they carry the delivery/doorman cluster and widen the class range)*
- **C14 — Restaurant owner.** Someone ate the food for years. Leans **A**.
- **C15 — Doorman / concierge / super.** Never actually saw anyone collect it. Leans **C/D**.
- **C16 — Delivery / gig driver (undocumented).** Mobile witness with real personal jeopardy — can “win” their game without solving anything (§7). Never saw the recipient.

### Belief, culture & community *(the other missing axis)*
- **C17 — Myth-keeper / online source-community moderator (the true believer).** Devoutly insists the journalist is real and heroic. **Direct opposite of C11** — built-in conflict. *(Conspiracy theorist #2.)*
- **C18 — Forensic-authorship / linguist expert** (promoted from document to PC; plausibly diaspora / multilingual-press background). Style analysis argues **B vs C** — keeps the mystery balanced.
- **C19 — Faith / mutual-aid community figure** (pastor / imam / rabbi, or a librarian the journalist confided in). A different value system entirely: truth, forgiveness, protecting people over the scoop.
- **C20 — AI artist** who collaborated with the “journalist” remotely and believes it was an AI partner all along. **Champions C.**

## Diversity axes now covered
- **Stake / relationship:** family, lover, mentor, protégé, worker, believer, community — not only investigators.
- **Class & occupation:** hedge-fund/exec → organizer, restaurant owner, doorman, undocumented driver, librarian.
- **Belief & ideology:** AI-doomer, evangelist/exec, populist debunker, devout believer, labor-left, faith/community.
- **Age & background:** young protégé → retired mentor; immigrant gig worker; diaspora authorship expert.
