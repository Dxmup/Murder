# V2 Candidate Fact Clusters

These are design buckets, not finished facts. Each cluster must eventually contain bounded firsthand observations distributed across at least three characters, with both supportive and weakening interpretations for the first three endings.

| Cluster | Central uncertainty | Likely holders |
|---|---|---|
| R's earliest appearance | Human founder, newsroom invention, or synthetic backfill? | Frankie, Tessa, Farah |
| Shared production | Collaboration around R or proof R was a mantle? | Tessa, Maren, Jules |
| Succession | Legitimate inheritance, AI-selected front, forgery, or Vale trap? | Jules, Tessa, Samira, Kit |
| Embodiment | Human R, paid performance, mistaken witness, or synthetic media? | Edi, Frankie, optional Ash |
| Parallax access | Ordinary project, unauthorized deployment, or R infrastructure? | Mira, Eli, Celeste |
| Autonomous behavior | Scripted automation, adaptive system, or human operators? | Eli, Edi, Mira, Kit |
| Physical logistics | One customer, several collectors, automated errands, or unrelated traffic? | Rafi, Dee, Manny, Inez |
| Vale's investigation | What did Vale seek, claim, fabricate, or discover? | Samira, Morgan, Jules, Eli |
| Vale's final movements | Harm, voluntary disappearance, staged trail, or invented narrative? | Samira, Morgan, Dee, Inez |
| Article rumor | Vale's ruse, R's ruse, AI manipulation, institutional tactic, or accretion? | Graham, Arden, Celeste, Mira |
| Financial and political reaction | Evidence of a plot or people exploiting uncertainty? | Graham, Mira, Arden, Celeste |
| The reckoning message | Human invitation, mantle act, autonomous send, spoof, or scheduled relic? | Arden, Eli, Jules, Kit |

No cluster may contain a complete article, verified human R, dispositive AI confession, or conclusive account of Vale's fate.
