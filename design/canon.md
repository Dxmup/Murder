# Locked Canon

Running list of canonical decisions locked during design (things §3–§13 of the handoff left open). Everything here should be treated as fixed and propagated into character sheets, evidence, messages, and the app.

## The byline: **“R.”**
The journalist publishes under the single-initial byline **“R.”** — pieces read *“by R.”*
- **Why:** a byline that is almost *nothing* — maximally Satoshi/Dread-Pirate-Roberts hollow, impossible to attach to a face (reinforces F003 no-photo, and keeps A/B/C/D all live). It also echoes the game's title, *How Many R's in Murder?*, and the theme of disputed simple answers.
- **Usage:** every reference to the journalist's public identity uses “R.” Characters may speculate what the “R” stands for (a name? “reporter”? nothing?) — that speculation is in-fiction and never resolved.
- **Community/lore hook:** C17's fan community (“The Questionry”) and the charity donations signed *“Questions matter”* (PF09) orbit the mystique of “R.”
