## Structural checks

- Every core character is the initial holder of at least one fact.
- No primary ending depends on an optional character.
- No fact verifies a complete article, a human R, Vale as R, or Vale's death.
- No single character holds the complete corpus arc: Frankie holds early, Tessa middle, Edi late.
- No single character holds more than one of the three escalating Vale incidents: Samira, Morgan, and Dee each hold one.
- Eli owns no origin-cluster facts and no longer owns the physical intervention record; the Ending 4 keys still cross three distinct social knots.
