## Shared public chronology

Only the following broad sequence is currently locked:

1. Reporting attributed to R appears for roughly fifteen years.
2. Human memories, collective work, performed embodiment, and automated behavior accumulate around the identity.
3. A rumor spreads that R is preparing a major AI investigation. No complete article is authenticated.
4. Vale investigates, imitates, courts, or otherwise approaches the R identity.
5. Vale disappears before the anniversary gathering.
6. An R-associated account promises a reckoning at the event.
7. The room constructs a verdict from incomplete evidence.

Exact dates, events, and custody chains remain to be designed in the V2 timeline. They must be compatible with all four rows above. The rumor may originate with Vale, R, an AI, an interested institution, or emergent repetition.
