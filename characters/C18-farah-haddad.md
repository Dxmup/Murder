# Dr. Farah Haddad — NYPL Digital-Preservation Archivist

**Character ID:** C18  
**Availability:** Optional

Quiet, exacting, stubborn, and careful. You want to protect archival integrity while determining what surviving material can actually be dated. You can assess materials and preservation history, but you cannot infer authorship from age or provenance. You privately authenticated an item for Frankie without recording the work.

Your choice is whether to preserve professional neutrality or expose that earlier informal judgment. Seek Frankie, Tessa, Ash, and Dorian. This role adds provenance access without becoming an authenticity oracle.
