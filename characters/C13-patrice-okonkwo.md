## Character Background

You did not come up through journalism or tech. You came up through neighborhoods — church basements, tenant meetings, the slow arithmetic of votes. What you learned there is that the *story* a city tells about an event matters more than the event. R. is now a story with no author to control it, which means it is available. You intend to be the one who frames it.

Tonight the room is thick with people who each want R. to have been one specific thing — a martyr, a hoax, a machine, a movement. You don't need R. to have been any of those. You need to be standing next to the version that's winning when the lights come up, wearing the word "reformer" like it was always yours.

There is a second reason you're here, and it lives lower in your chest. You have a name that could be attached to money you'd rather no one traced. You are working tonight, calmly, to get out in front of that before anyone else finds it.
