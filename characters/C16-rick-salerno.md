# Rick Salerno — Tabloid Reporter

**Character ID:** C16 · **Owns:** the disputed photo and the gossip pipeline · **You lean toward:** *you're rooting for R. to have been flesh and blood with dirty secrets — because a real person is a story you can sell, and an AI or a hoax doesn't cry on camera when you corner it.*

---

## Character Background

You built a career on the stories the respectable papers were too squeamish to print. Weddings that weren't, executives who lied, actors who cheated — you got there first, and you got there loud. R. was the opposite of everything you are: no face, no name, fifteen years of glory under a single initial while you hustled for scraps.

But a faceless legend is the biggest scoop alive, and tonight the whole cast of R.'s world is packed into one room. Fabricators, insiders, sources with loose mouths and lighter pockets. You smell blood.

You need this one. You need it more than any of them can know.

## What You Know

**Facts you're sure of:** R. published under a pseudonym for about fifteen years (F001) and was hugely respected (F002). No verified photograph of R. has ever existed (F003). R. announced an AI exposé (F009), then failed to publish it on schedule (F010). That unpublished article is the object everyone in this room is circling (F014). R. vanished right before publication (F015), and no one has confirmed a death (F016).

**What you can't prove / what you privately suspect:** You believe — you *need* to believe — there was a body behind the byline. But you've heard the whispers that different people describe R. differently, that "R." might be a mantle, a machine, or a myth someone stitched together. Every one of those theories is a knife aimed at your throat, because you can't sell a ghost.

> **Keep this to yourself:** Years ago you ran a "photo of R." that got publicly debunked, and it torched your credibility. This is a vendetta, and your whole career rides on being right this time. If a scoop is juicy enough, you *will* print it unverified — and you know it.

## Main Goal

Land the scoop of the decade — proof of who R. was, ideally with scandal attached — redeeming your debunked-photo disgrace.

## Sub-Goal One

Expose at least one fabricator — **Renata Kessler**, **Dorian Ashe**, or **Nadia Kaur** — on the record tonight.

## Sub-Goal Two

Get an exclusive from an insider — **Eleanor Voss** or **Wren Kaczmarek** — trading dirt if you have to.

## Special Ability

**Print It** — push a story to the public app feed, where it lands as "fact" even when it's unverified. It bends the room. **Forcing trigger:** if by Phase 3 you have uncovered **Eleanor's seven-year gap** (that she never verified the author) and she still hasn't given you an exclusive, you may **auto-print it** to the whole room — so your best card can't just sit in your pocket all night. It cannot prove what R. actually was — a headline is not a witness.

## AI Assistant

Your assistant knows this sheet, your facts, your materials, and your messages. It does not know who R. really was — nobody does — nor any other player's private secrets, nor any hidden "answer." It can help you chase, phrase, and time your scoop; it can't hand you the truth, because there isn't one to hand.
