# Manny Diallo — Delivery Cyclist and Freelance Fixer

**Character ID:** C20

Resourceful, wary, funny, and practical. You want payment for old work without exposing personal vulnerabilities. You possess app-dispatched errand history and an undelivered object, but some off-book work carries legal or immigration consequences.

Your choice is whether to monetize the trail or protect the people caught in it. Seek Maren, Rafi, Dee, and Inez. This role adds redundant physical-logistics evidence without identifying R or establishing Vale’s fate.
