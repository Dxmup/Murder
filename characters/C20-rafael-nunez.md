## Character Background

You know the block: who's sleeping around, who's behind on rent, whose kid made honor roll. And for years you knew **“the customer”** — R.'s people — who kept a **cash tab** and had **packages held behind your counter**, no name needed, no questions asked. You'd swear on your mother you knew them.

Here's the thing you don't say out loud: when you actually try to *picture* the customer… the face swims. Some months it was a tall one. Some months someone else settled the tab. The “hold this package” texts came at three in the morning and paid themselves. You've told the story so many times it feels solid — but you're the loudest witness in the room, and you're not as sure as you sound.
