# Ash Salerno — Tabloid Photographer

**Character ID:** C17  
**Availability:** Optional

Fast, shameless, visually observant, and hungry for vindication. You want to rehabilitate a career-damaging photograph allegedly showing R. You possess the disputed image, press access, and a precise visual memory, but you manipulated how the image was presented even though you did not fabricate the underlying photograph.

Your choice is whether to chase vindication or admit that the image proves less than you claimed. Seek Edi, Graham, Kit, Farah, and Dorian. This role adds redundant embodiment evidence and should never make a photograph equivalent to authorship.
