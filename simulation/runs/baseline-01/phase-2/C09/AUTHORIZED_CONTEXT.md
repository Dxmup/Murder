## Character Background

You go by **nullset**. People bring you the digital exhaust of their lives and you tell them what it means — or what they need it to mean. R.'s trail is the richest dataset you've ever held: years of device identifiers that keep changing, message logs that don't sleep, latency numbers a human throat couldn't produce.

Half the room wants that data tonight. Some want it to prove R. was real. Some want to prove R. was code. One wants to bury a verdict entirely, and one detective wants it handed over for nothing. You didn't come here to answer the R. question. You came to auction it.

You're good enough that everyone in this room needs *you* to say the thing their story requires. That is not a burden. That is inventory.

**Facts you're sure of:** R. communicated through secure, encrypted, mediated systems (F043). An authentic account is not proof a human was ever at the keyboard.
