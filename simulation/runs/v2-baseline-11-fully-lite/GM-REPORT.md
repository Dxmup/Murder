## What worked

- Voluntary object showing and transfer rules operated cleanly without forced authority or automated authentication (e.g. C02 showing O01, C04 showing O03, C08 showing O04).
- Article existence and fate ambiguity remained stable across sections, satisfying design intent.
- Quiet-role and institutional characters successfully negotiated bounded commitments, neighborhood protection pacts, and concrete written agreements.

## What broke

- Structural weight imbalance: Human Mantle overcrowded One Human and Synthetic Origin by the final ballot count.
- Progressive difficulty threshold proved too high, leaving Kit Rakes (C15) isolated as a minoritarian voice and failing to seed emergent synthesis across other agents.
- Incomplete evidence chains in ballot supports, with agents frequently omitting key dimensions like collaborative_production or transition.

## Character findings

- C15 (Kit Rakes) persistently championed the progressive narrative across all three sections but failed to convert peers due to strong anchoring on Human Mantle.
- C01 (Arden Bell) and C02 (Frankie Lowell) anchored human/mantle continuity by repeatedly citing foundational evidence V2F001, V2F002, and V2F003.
- Institutional characters like C09 (Rafi Calderón), C10 (Dee), and C16 (Inez) successfully secured concrete local defense and privacy boundaries.

## Mechanic findings

- Deterministic ballot distribution successfully served as a reliable measurement of agent convergence rather than a hidden truth.
- Voluntary transfer mechanics successfully prevented forced custody while enabling rich face-to-face negotiations.
- Early belief weights and investigative leak flows heavily favored collective Night Desk labor evidence, creating an irreversible momentum toward Human Mantle.

## Priority changes

- Recalibrate agent prompt priors and evidentiary weights to give Synthetic Origin and One Human equal competitive footing against Human Mantle.
- Strengthen the distinct Vale incident and historical ordering mechanics so that the Progressive pathway achieves higher structural parity in final ballots.
- Introduce stronger synthetic provenance ambiguity markers earlier in Section 2 to prevent premature consensus on human mantle and voluntary disappearance.
