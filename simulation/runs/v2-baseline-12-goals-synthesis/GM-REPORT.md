## Overall verdict

The simulation successfully maintained mechanical integrity, voluntary object transfer protocols, and high ambiguity around the article and Vale's fate, but suffered from strong social convergence toward the 'human mantle' ballot outcome and severe suppression of the progressive identity path.

## What worked

- Voluntary object showing and custody transfers (O01, O03, O04, O07b) operated flawlessly without forced transfer or immunity mechanics.
- Article ambiguity and existence belief means remained stable and uncertain across sections (hovering around 38-41%).
- Characters demonstrated robust agency, active negotiation of secondary goals, and stable bilateral partnerships.
- Mechanical integrity across ballot aggregation and voting protocols performed reliably.

## What broke

- Severe suppression of the progressive identity path, garnering only 1 vote (C15, Kit Rakes) due to high ordering difficulty and lack of multi-character collaborative synthesis.
- Premature social convergence and administrative tidying around the 'human mantle' hypothesis, driven heavily by collaborative routing slips (V2F035) overshadowing synthetic origin and technical logs.
- Widespread incomplete evidence chains and uncited evidence dimensions among character ballot selections.

## Character findings

- C15 (Kit Rakes) acted as an isolated outlier advocating the progressive four-stage evolutionary arc, which other characters treated with skepticism or rejected in favor of collaborative human-centric models.
- C01 (Arden Bell) and C16 (Inz Baptiste) successfully secured institutional survival and neighborhood partnerships while favoring the human mantle outcome.
- C05 (Mira Sen) and C12 (Graham Pike) championed synthetic origin arguments but failed to attract sufficient evidentiary backing from peers.
- C13 (Samira Vale) transcripts actively corrected false assumptions regarding corporate murder, aligning with the prevalent 'voluntarily_vanished' ballot consensus.

## Mechanic findings

- Deterministic ballot distributions successfully measured agent divergence and convergence without overriding agent autonomy.
- The voluntary object showing mechanism provided effective face-to-face verification touchpoints without triggering forced disclosures or authentication exploits.
- Evidence dimension tracking exposed structural disconnects between agent reasoning summaries and cited evidence IDs (e.g., missing collaborative production and identity authority dimensions).
- The separate Vale incident successfully preserved ambiguity, preventing police authority or forced murder endings.

## Priority changes

- Adjust agent prompt weights or scoring incentives for the progressive identity path to ensure equal structural weight with human mantle, one human, and synthetic origin.
- Introduce disruptive events or conflicting investigative revelations in Section 3 to prevent premature consensus and administrative tidying among agents.
- Strengthen intermediate investigative touchpoints across multiple non-Kit characters so that the multi-stage historical transition can be collaboratively pieced together.
- Enforce stricter validation between agent belief reasoning summaries and cited evidence dimensions in future simulation runs.
