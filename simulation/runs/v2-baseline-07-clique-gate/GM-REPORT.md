# Simulation Game Master Report

## Overall verdict

The simulation successfully demonstrated exceptional character agency, reciprocal face-to-face negotiations, and an appropriately unresolved mystery regarding the article's existence. However, it failed to maintain structural parity across primary endings, resulting in a severe premature convergence on the 'human_mantle' and 'voluntarily_vanished' outcomes due to evidentiary imbalances, herd dynamics, non-diegetic prompt leaks, and an overly restrictive progressive gate threshold.

## Ending and gate result

The simulation resulted in an overwhelming 'human_mantle' identity consensus (11 of 16 ballots) and a 'voluntarily_vanished' fate consensus (12 of 16 ballots). The progressive gate remained closed (eligible: false) because the social threshold requiring a 3-agent investigator clique to vote for both progressive identity and AI-caused death was unmet, despite all physical progressive keys being globally unlocked.

## What worked

- High-fidelity face-to-face social circulation where local characters (C09, C10, C16) established protective boundaries to resist unilateral extraction by investigators.
- Quiet-role local coordination and defense, allowing characters to achieve independent victory conditions (such as C10 and C16 locking down the camera gap V2F026 explanation to shield residents) decoupled from macro ending beliefs.
- The article's existence and fate remained successfully unresolved and stable, with aggregate belief means remaining below 40% across all three sections.
- Factual progressive gate keys (identity_history, autonomous_control, vale_incident) were successfully unlocked globally and remained resilient against false-positive votes like Arden's analytical misattribution.

## What broke

- Severe structural imbalance across primary endings, with 68.75% of agents converging on 'human_mantle' and 75% on 'voluntarily_vanished', violating the locked design intent of equal weight.
- Complete failure of all 16 agents to compile a full evidence chain (full_evidence_chain: false), demonstrating systemic cognitive deficits in linking complex continuous dimensions.
- Functional impossibility of coordinating the progressive ending under isolated execution constraints, as the high 3-investigator clique threshold decoupled the progressive gate from the factual clues.
- Non-diegetic prompt leakage where system administrative corrections (clip and printer monitor notifications on typography and bodega ledgers) bypassed character agency and manifested as direct character dialogue.

## Character findings

- Kit Rakes (C15) exhibited dogmatic, loud advocacy of the progressive narrative that triggered epistemic skepticism in others, while Kit's subsequent retractions of disproved sub-theories (the typography and bodega-cipher V2F007) ruined their credibility.
- Arden Bell (C01) committed analytical misattribution, voting for a 'progressive' ending while citing exclusively 'human_mantle' collaborative facts (V2F007, V2F008, V2F037, V2F042) and selecting a 'voluntarily_vanished' fate.
- Morgan Shaw (C14) demonstrated outstanding investigative agency by uncovering a manufactured client-supplied route lead (V2F027) and transparently disclosing the timeline contamination in Section 3.
- Eli Navarro (C06) successfully decoupled Parallax infrastructure capability from system autonomy and intent, which served as a crucial logical block that anchored other characters to a human mantle consensus.

## Mechanic findings

- The progressive gate's social threshold (3-agent investigator clique voting progressive and AI death) is too high and statistically decoupled from isolated agent behaviors, preventing the progressive path from being an achievable gameplay state.
- Object custody remained completely static, as all actions regarding O01 and O03 were 'show' rather than 'transfer', preventing objects from functioning as dynamic tokens of shifting trust or leverage.
- Unequal evidentiary distribution and ease of synthesis for collaborative-production clues (V2F004, V2F005, V2F007, V2F008) generated a strong narrative gravity that pulled agents to default to Human Mantle when individual chains were incomplete.

## Priority changes

- Diegetize system-level corrections by converting administrative notifications (e.g., typographical adjustments or tax-code audits) into physical, in-world clues to prevent immersion breaking.
- Reduce the progressive gate's social coordination threshold from a 3-agent investigator clique to a highly motivated duo (2 agents) to make it achievable under isolated execution.
- Rebalance primary ending weights by strengthening and simplifying clues for 'one_human' (e.g., singular physical signatures) and 'synthetic_origin' (e.g., pre-byline machine footprints) to make them competitive against 'human_mantle'.
- Introduce mechanics that explicitly reward or require the physical transfer of object custody (allowing other characters to privately analyze objects) to enable true reciprocal circulation of physical evidence.
